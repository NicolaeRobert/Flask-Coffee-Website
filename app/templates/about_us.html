Our Story

How it begin:

It all started with a trip where we tasted a coffee that completely changed our mornings. We wanted to recreate that perfect balance of body, acidity, and aroma — so we began learning every step, from sourcing beans to roasting each batch by hand.

What we do today:

We roast in small batches to preserve freshness, work directly with farmers, and constantly test roast profiles to bring out the unique character of each variety. Every bag tells a story — region, altitude, processing — all influencing what ends up in your cup.

Why it matters to us:

For us, coffee isn't just a product — it's a bridge between people. We support sustainable practices, pay farmers fairly, and invest in long-term partnerships. When you drink our coffee, you're part of a small change that makes a big difference.


Timeline / Milestones

2016: First roast in a garage — pure experimentation.

2018: Opened our roastery and began direct trade with two farms.

2021: Launched our single-origin and specialty coffee line.

2024: Introduced sustainable, fully recyclable packaging.
